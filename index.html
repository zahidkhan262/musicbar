Arijit Singh

Arijit Singh (born 25 April 1987) is an Indian playback singer. He sings predominantly in Hindi and Bengali but has also performed in various other Indian languages He is the recipient of a National Award and a record six Filmfare Awards.

Atif Aslam

Muhammad Atif Aslam (Urdu: محمد عاطف اسلم‎; born 12 March 1983; mostly referred to as Atif Aslam) is a Pakistani playback singer and actor. He has recorded numerous chart-topping songs in both Pakistan and India and is known for his vocal belting technique.

Sunidhhi Chauhan

Sunidhi Chauhan (pronounced [sʊnɪd̪i tʃʰoːhən]; born 14 August 1983) is an Indian playback singer. Born in Delhi, she began performing in local gatherings at the age of four and made her career debut at the age of 12, with the film Shastra (1996).
